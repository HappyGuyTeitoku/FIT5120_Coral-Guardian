What is Nutrient Pollution?

Nutrient pollution occurs when an excess of nutrients, primarily nitrogen and phosphorus, enter water bodies.

These nutrients, often from sources like argricultural runoff, sewage and industrial waste, act as fertilizers in the water, leading to the overgrowth of algae. This excessive algae growth can harm water quality, disrupt aquatic ecosystems, and create dead zones where life cannot survive.
