Introduction

Why Reducing Nutrient Pollution Matters for Melbourne?

Nutrient pollution, such as phosphorus and nitrogen, is having a profound impact on Melbourne's water quality, affecting various aspects of our daily lives.

These pollutants come not only from agriculture and industry but also from the everyday cleaning products and household items we use. To effectively protect our water resources, we must first understand the sources and impacts of this pollution and make environmentally friendly choices in our daily lives.
